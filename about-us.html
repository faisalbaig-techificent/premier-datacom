About Premier DataCom

Building Trust Through Smart Technology Systems Since 2005

Premier DataCom, Inc. traces its roots back to 2005 in Austin, TX. What began as a small, dedicated team with a vision to deliver quality structured infrastructure has grown into one of Texas’s most trusted technology construction companies. With decades of successful project experience, the company has built a reputation on reliability, technical excellence, and long-standing client relationships.

For over 20 years, we have proudly delivered structured cabling solutions and data center construction services, building high-performance networks for regional, national, and global companies. From small-scale installations to large, complex technology and communication systems, our team works together to design, deploy, and support networks that are as unique as our clients. Our mission is focused on driving results—leveraging expertise, attention to detail, and unwavering professionalism.

At Premier, we are known not just for honoring commitments—but for meeting them with the highest level of integrity while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism. We don’t just complete projects—we build partnerships that are responsive, collaborative, and built to last, ensuring long-term success.

Innovation is at the heart of what we do. Our ability to adapt and evolve with the rapidly changing demands of the technology landscape has allowed us to offer tailored, high-performance solutions to each client. By working closely with customers, designers, subcontractors, consultants, and suppliers, we ensure a streamlined workflow and deliver exceptional results tailored to fit each team.

Our team is composed of experienced professionals who understand the intricacies of modern construction environments. Whether it’s a commercial office, hospital, school, industrial site, or data center, we approach every project with the same dedication to precision, safety, and scalability.

As we move forward, Premier DataCom continues to embrace emerging technologies and industry best practices to stay ahead of the curve. Our mission remains clear: to provide dependable, innovative, and customized technology construction solutions that empower our clients' success—now and into the future.
